Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ added successfully!

1. What is Medica?

Medica is a platform that provides accurate and timely medical test reporting to patients and healthcare providers, helping them access essential health information securely.

2. How can I access my test results?

You can access your test results by logging into the Patient Portal after registering with us.

3. Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we use advanced encryption methods to ensure your personal and medical data is kept secure and private at all times.

4. How can I request an appointment for a test?

You can request an appointment by visiting the 'Request Appointment' section in the 'Find Test' part of our website.

5. What should I do if I have a complaint about the report?

If you have a complaint, please contact us via the 'Feedback/Complaints' section on the homepage. We will address your concern promptly.
