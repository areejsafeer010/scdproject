Payment for Test Reports

To make a payment for your test report, kindly follow the steps below:

Step 1: Select Payment Method

Choose your preferred payment method: Credit/Debit Card, PayPal, or Bank Transfer.

Step 2: Enter Report ID

You will find the unique report ID on your test report. Enter it here to proceed.

Step 3: Payment Confirmation

Once you confirm your payment, you will receive a confirmation email and the test results will be sent to you.
